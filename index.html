📚 Complete User Guide

🎯 Overview

This all-in-one tool helps you create, optimize, and analyze content using Google's E-E-A-T framework and AI visibility best practices.

📊 Feature 1: Compare URLs

Purpose: Analyze your content vs competitors to find ranking opportunities.

  1. Enter your URL
  2. Enter 1-3 competitor URLs (one per line)
  3. Click "Analyze"
  4. Get E-E-A-T scores, specific sections to rewrite, before/after examples, and recommendations

✨ Feature 2: Rewrite Content

Purpose: Optimize existing content for better rankings.

  1. Enter your target keyword
  2. Select page type
  3. Paste your current content
  4. Click "Rewrite"

🚀 Feature 3: Generate Content

Purpose: Create new, high-quality content from scratch.

  1. Enter primary keyword, secondary keywords
  2. Select page type and target audience
  3. Describe your brand
  4. Click "Generate"

✅ E-E-A-T Checklist

  • Author name and bio with credentials
  • Author LinkedIn link
  • 3+ authoritative citations per 1000 words
  • Original data, case studies, or examples
  • HTTPS (secured site)
  • Clear contact information
  • Privacy policy / Terms of Service
  • Last updated date visible
  • Company About page
Pro Tips

Start with Compare URLs — understand your competition first.

Expand each "Section to Rewrite" card to see exactly what to change and why.

Use the Copy Suggestion button to grab the rewritten text instantly.
If you get an error: Check your internet connection and try again. The AI sometimes takes longer on complex prompts — wait a moment and retry.
