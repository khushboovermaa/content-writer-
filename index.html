📚 Complete User Guide

🎯 Overview

This all-in-one tool helps you create, optimize, and analyze content using Google's E-E-A-T framework and AI visibility best practices.

📊 Feature 1: Compare URLs

Purpose: Analyze your content vs competitors to find ranking opportunities.

How to use:

  1. Enter your URL
  2. Enter 1-3 competitor URLs (one per line)
  3. Click "Analyze"
  4. Get E-E-A-T scores, gaps, and recommendations

What you get: E-E-A-T breakdown, content quality metrics, key strengths, gaps, and specific recommendations to outrank them.

✨ Feature 2: Rewrite Content

Purpose: Optimize existing content for better rankings.

How to use:

  1. Enter your target keyword
  2. Select page type (Blog, Service, Homepage, Case Study)
  3. Paste your current content
  4. Click "Rewrite"
  5. Copy and customize the optimized version

Optimizations applied:

  • E-E-A-T signals (credentials, citations, expertise)
  • AI Overview structure (answer-first, question headers)
  • Keyword optimization
  • Technical improvements (schema, links, hierarchy)

🚀 Feature 3: Generate Content

Purpose: Create new, high-quality content from scratch.

How to use:

  1. Enter primary keyword
  2. Add secondary keywords (optional)
  3. Select page type
  4. Enter target audience
  5. Describe your brand/company
  6. Click "Generate"
  7. Copy and customize the output

Output includes: Complete content with E-E-A-T signals, AI optimization, proper structure, meta tags, FAQ, and content score.

✅ E-E-A-T Checklist

  • Author name and bio with credentials
  • Author LinkedIn link
  • 3+ authoritative citations per 1000 words
  • Original data, case studies, or examples
  • HTTPS (secured site)
  • Clear contact information
  • Privacy policy/Terms of Service
  • Last updated date visible
  • Company About page

🎯 Content Strategy

Best workflow:

  1. Compare URLs - Understand what it takes to rank
  2. Identify gaps - Note missing elements
  3. Generate or Rewrite - Create optimized content
  4. Customize - Add your brand voice and real examples
  5. Publish & Monitor - Track improvements

📝 AI Overview Optimization

Structure every piece for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity:

  1. Opening: Direct answer (1-2 sentences)
  2. Why it matters: Context and value
  3. Main body: Detailed explanation with facts/stats
  4. Practical tips: Actionable takeaways
  5. FAQ: Answer follow-up questions
  6. CTA: Next step for reader
